Company Leave Policy

Employees are eligible for 15 days of paid time off per year.
Unused leave can be carried forward up to 5 days into the next year.
Sick leave is provided separately and includes 10 days per year.
Employees must apply for leave at least 3 days in advance.
Emergency leave can be requested without prior notice but requires manager approval.
For extended leave requests beyond 10 days, employees must contact HR.
Leave balance can be checked through the internal HR portal.
